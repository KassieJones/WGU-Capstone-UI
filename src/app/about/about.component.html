The Benefits of Reading!

Reading is one of the single most powerful workouts for the brain, according to research done on the topic.

The National Library of Medicine has confirmed via MRI scans that reading stimulates and strengthens the complex neurological network within the human brain.

Some of the ways it does this are:

Increasing vocabulary and comprehension

Increasing the ability to empathize

Increasing concentration and memory retention

Increasing the amount and depth of knowledge held

Increasing critical thinking and analytical abilities
